Operations Overview
22 months · Apr 2024 – Mar 2026 · Received → Laid → Cut → Sorted
Flow summary: Fabric is received, laid (often drawing from stock), cut, then sorted. Laying exceeds receiving by 3.6% avg — operation draws stock regularly. Cutting exceeds laying by 2.4% — carryover fabric. Sorting averages 0.5% loss vs cut (worsening recently). Performance improved 2× from 2024 (31%) to 2025 (64%) — a step-change at Jan-25.

Performance vs Target
How each stage measures against monthly target · target quality varies significantly
Target context: 2024 targets (1–2.5M/month) were set 2–5× above operational capacity — the operation never cut more than 540k units in any month. From Jan-25, targets were recalibrated to 300–800k range. This makes pre-2025 performance percentages (10–53%) not directly comparable to post-2025 (53–79%). The absolute cut volume is the most reliable comparator across the full period.
